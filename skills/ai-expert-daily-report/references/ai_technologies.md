# AI Technologies Reference

## Foundation Models

### Large Language Models (LLMs)

#### GPT Series (OpenAI)
- **GPT-4**: Multimodal capabilities, reasoning, 128K context window
- **GPT-4 Turbo**: Optimized for speed and cost
- **GPT-4 Vision**: Image understanding and analysis
- **Key Features**: Few-shot learning, chain-of-thought reasoning, tool use

#### Claude Family (Anthropic)
- **Claude 3 Opus**: SOTA capabilities, 200K context
- **Claude 3 Sonnet**: Balanced performance and cost
- **Claude 3 Haiku**: Fast, cost-effective
- **Key Focus**: Constitutional AI, safety alignment, reduced hallucination

#### LLaMA Family (Meta)
- **LLaMA 2 70B**: Open-source SOTA performance
- **LLaMA 2 Chat**: Fine-tuned for dialogue
- **Key Features**: Open access, strong community support, efficient inference

#### Chinese AI Models
- **Baidu ERNIE 4.0**: Leading Chinese language understanding
- **Alibaba Qwen (通义千问)**: 72B parameter model, strong bilingual capabilities
- **Tencent Hunyuan (混元)**: Multimodal, enterprise-focused
- **Zhipu AI ChatGLM**: Open-source Chinese-English bilingual models

### Multimodal Models

#### Image Generation
- **DALL-E 3**: High-quality image generation from text
- **Midjourney v6**: Photorealistic generation, artistic style control
- **Stable Diffusion XL**: Open-source, high customizability
- **Ideogram**: Excellent text rendering in images

#### Video Generation
- **Runway Gen-2**: Text-to-video, video editing
- **Pika Labs**: Fast video generation, style control
- **Sora (OpenAI)**: Long-form video generation (upcoming)

#### Audio Generation
- **ElevenLabs**: Text-to-speech, voice cloning
- **MusicLM (Google)**: Text-to-music generation
- **Stable Audio**: Music and sound effect generation

## AI Safety and Alignment

### Safety Research
- **Constitutional AI**: Anthropic's approach to safety through principles
- **Red Teaming**: Systematic adversarial testing
- **AI Alignment**: Ensuring AI goals match human values
- **Interpretability**: Understanding model decision-making

### Alignment Techniques
- **RLHF**: Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback
- **Constitutional AI**: Principle-based training
- **Preference Learning**: Learning from human preferences
- **Constitutional Principles**: Harmlessness, honesty, helpfulness

### Ethical Considerations
- **Bias Mitigation**: Reducing training data biases
- **Fairness**: Ensuring equitable AI outcomes
- **Transparency**: Open-source models, interpretability
- **Privacy**: Federated learning, differential privacy

## AI Research Landscape

### Top Conferences
- **NeurIPS**: Neural Information Processing Systems
- **ICML**: International Conference on Machine Learning
- **ICLR**: International Conference on Learning Representations
- **ACL**: Association for Computational Linguistics
- **CVPR**: Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition
- **AAAI**: Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence

### Key Research Areas
- **Scaling Laws**: Understanding model size vs. performance
- **Emergent Abilities**: Unexpected capabilities in large models
- **Efficient AI**: Smaller models, fewer parameters, faster inference
- **Multimodal Learning**: Integrating text, image, audio, video
- **Reasoning**: Complex problem-solving, planning, logic
- **World Models**: Understanding physical world dynamics

### Research Trends
- **LLM Scaling**: Continuing to push model size limits
- **Efficient Inference**: Quantization, distillation, pruning
- **Agent Systems**: AI agents with tool use and planning
- **Embodied AI**: Robotics, physical world interaction
- **AI for Science**: Accelerating scientific discovery
