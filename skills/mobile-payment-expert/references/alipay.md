# Alipay (支付宝) Reference

## Platform Overview

**Founded**: 2004
**Parent Company**: Ant Group (Alibaba)
**Market Share**: ~45-50% of Chinese mobile payment market
**User Base**: Over 1 billion monthly active users
**Merchant Base**: Over 60 million merchants

## Core Features

### QR Code Payment (二维码支付)
- **Dynamic QR**: User-generated QR codes for receiving payments
- **Static QR**: Merchant QR codes displayed at POS
- **Scan to Pay**: Users scan merchant QR to pay
- **Payment Code**: Users show QR for merchants to scan

### Touch Payment (碰一下)
- **NFC-Based**: Near Field Communication technology
- **Tap-to-Pay**: Simply tap phone to POS to complete payment
- **No App Opening**: Works even if Alipay app is not open
- **One-Step Payment**: Faster and more convenient than QR code

### Alipay+ (Alipay Plus)
- **International Expansion**: Alipay services in overseas markets
- **Multi-Currency**: Support for multiple currencies
- **Local Partnerships**: Integration with local payment providers
- **Tourist-Friendly**: Cross-border payment solutions for travelers

### Alipay Mini Programs (支付宝小程序)
- **Seamless Integration**: Built-in payment flow within Mini Programs
- **Service Ecosystem**: Wide range of services and utilities
- **One-Click Payment**: Saved payment methods for quick checkout
- **Merchant-Specific**: Customized payment experiences

## Touch Payment (碰一下) Deep Dive

### Technology Overview
- **NFC Technology**: Uses Near Field Communication for contactless payment
- **Fast Transaction**: Sub-second transaction time
- **Universal Compatibility**: Works with most modern smartphones
- **Secure**: Encrypted transaction, tokenization

### User Experience
- **Convenience**: Simply tap phone, no need to open app
- **Speed**: Faster than QR code scanning
- **Reliability**: Works even with weak internet connection
- **Accessibility**: Easy to use for all demographics

### Merchant Benefits
- **Faster Checkout**: Reduced queue times
- **Better UX**: Smoother customer experience
- **Lower Friction**: One-step payment process
- **Cost-Effective**: Compatible with existing NFC POS terminals

### Adoption Status (2024-2025)
- **Rollout Phase**: Expanding to more merchants and regions
- **Key Locations**: Major cities, retail chains, transportation
- **Merchant Support**: POS terminals upgraded for touch payment
- **User Education**: Marketing campaigns to promote usage

### Use Cases
- **Retail**: Supermarkets, convenience stores, shopping malls
- **Transportation**: Subway, buses, taxis
- **Food & Beverage**: Restaurants, cafes, fast food
- **Entertainment**: Cinemas, theme parks, events
