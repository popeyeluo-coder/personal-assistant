# WeChat Pay (微信支付) Reference

## Platform Overview

**Founded**: 2013
**Parent Company**: Tencent
**Market Share**: ~40-45% of Chinese mobile payment market
**User Base**: Over 1 billion monthly active users
**Merchant Base**: Over 50 million merchants

## Core Features

### QR Code Payment (二维码支付)
- **Dynamic QR**: User-generated QR codes for receiving payments
- **Static QR**: Merchant QR codes displayed at POS
- **Scan to Pay**: Users scan merchant QR to pay
- **Payment Code**: Users show QR for merchants to scan

### Mini Program Payments (小程序支付)
- **Seamless Integration**: Built-in payment flow within Mini Programs
- **One-Click Payment**: Saved payment methods for quick checkout
- **Subscription Payments**: Recurring billing for subscriptions
- **Merchant-Specific**: Customized payment experiences

### WeChat Pay Score (微信支付分)
- **Credit Scoring**: AI-powered credit assessment based on user behavior
- **Pay First, Refund Later**: Use services before payment
- **Rentals**: Bike sharing, phone rentals, power banks
- **Trust Services**: Hotel check-in without deposit

### In-App Payments (应用内支付)
- **Third-Party Apps**: Payment integration in external apps
- **WeChat Ecosystem**: Games, official accounts, services
- **Social Payments**: Red packets, transfers, group payments

## Merchant Solutions

### Smart POS (智能POS)
- **All-in-One Device**: Payment, receipt printing, inventory management
- **Multi-Payment Support**: WeChat Pay, Alipay, cash, cards
- **Cloud Management**: Remote device management and reporting
- **Analytics**: Sales data, customer insights, trends

### Merchant Management (商户管理)
- **Dashboard**: Sales overview, transaction history, reconciliation
- **Staff Management**: Employee accounts, permissions, tracking
- **Multi-Store**: Chain store management, centralized control
- **Marketing Tools**: Coupons, discounts, loyalty programs

### Marketing and Promotion (营销推广)
- **Red Packets (红包)**: Digital lucky red packets for engagement
- **Coupons (优惠券)**: Digital coupons and vouchers
- **Membership (会员卡)**: Digital membership cards
- **Social Sharing**: Share promotions on Moments

## Ecosystem Integration

### WeChat Pay Plus (微信支付+)

## Merchant Adoption Trends

### Retail Industry
- **High Adoption**: Almost universal adoption in retail
- **Use Cases**: Supermarkets, convenience stores, malls, restaurants
- **Benefits**: Faster checkout, reduced cash handling, better customer experience
- **Challenges**: Transaction fees, POS equipment costs, integration

### Service Industry
- **Growing Adoption**: Increasing adoption in services
- **Use Cases**: Restaurants, cafes, salons, entertainment
- **Benefits**: Tips, customer engagement, loyalty programs
- **Challenges**: Customization needs, staff training

### Online Commerce
- **Universal Adoption**: Standard for e-commerce
- **Use Cases**: E-commerce platforms, social commerce, direct sales
- **Benefits**: Seamless checkout, mobile-first experience
- **Challenges**: Platform fees, competition from other payment methods
